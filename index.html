Data Units Explained

  • Tourism Receipts
    Unit: USD
    The total income a country earns from international visitors, measured in USD.
  • Tourism Arrivals
    Unit: Number of People (Bubble Size)
    The total number of international tourists a country receives.
  • Tourism Exports
    Unit: % of Total Exports
    Shows how vital tourism income is to the country's entire export economy.
  • Tourism Departures
    Unit: Number of People
    The number of residents from that country who travel abroad for tourism.
  • Tourism Expenditures
    Unit: % of Total Imports
    The share of a country's import spending that goes towards its citizens traveling abroad.
  • Inflation
    Unit: Annual % Change
    The rate of price increases within the country.
  • Unemployment
    Unit: % of Labor Force
    The percentage of the workforce that is unemployed.
